About Minty AI

Minty AI is an AI developed by Ahmed Aleid and Mohammed Albaiz. Its main goal is to help you understand the curriculum in a clearer and simpler way.

Supported subjects:

  • English Grammar Book
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • ICT

Fun Facts

  • Minty AI is named after Mohammed’s mint plant, Minty.
  • This project originally started last year, not because of WAICY. The idea came back after seeing WAICY announce the 2025 competition, which encouraged us to update it and improve it.
