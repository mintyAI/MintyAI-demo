Commonly Asked Questions

Question: Why don't I use the normal ChatGPT instead? Normal AI answers are often too broad, too complex, or off-curriculum. MintyAI is trained to explain simply **while staying aligned with the 9th-grade curriculum**.

Question: Is MintyAI 100% accurate? No AI is perfect — but MintyAI is tuned for high accuracy and curriculum-correct explanations most of the time.

Question: Does this project gain any profit? No. The project is entirely free and has no monetization of any kind.

Question: Does your project get updated? Yes! We update it regularly to improve accuracy, visuals, and usability. Your feedback helps — submit it at mintyai.xyz/feedback.html.
