About Minty AI

Minty AI is an AI developed by Ahmed Aleid and Mohammed Albaiz. Its main goal is to help you understand the curriculum in a clearer and simpler way.

Supported subjects:

  • English Grammar Book
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • ICT
  • Math
  • World History
  • Physics (will be added VERY soon)

Fun Facts

  • Minty AI is named after Mohammed’s mint plant, Minty.
  • A similar project was developed by Ahmed last year, but decided to renew it because of WAICY's 2025 competition.

Commonky asked questions

  • Question: Why don't I use the normal ChatGPT instead?
    Answer: Because normal AI answers are too broad or too specific, along with being complicated in general. But MintyAI solves both since it's trained to explain in simpler ways while trained to not go far from the curriculum.
  • Question: Is MintyAI 100% accurate?
    Answer: No, since at the end of the day it's still and AI model, but it's very accurate most if the time.
